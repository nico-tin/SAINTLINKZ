How to Measure

  1. Use a soft measuring tape or flexible string
  2. Wrap around wrist bone where you'd wear the bracelet
  3. Keep it snug but comfortable - not too tight!
  4. Note the measurement in inches or cm
  5. Add 0.5 - 1 inch for comfort and movement
  6. Compare to our size chart above
