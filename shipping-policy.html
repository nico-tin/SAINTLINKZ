Shipping & Payment Policy

Shipping Information

Shipping Fees: Customer bears all shipping charges. Fees depend on location and weight.

Payment Required: Full payment is required before production begins.

Cancellation Policy

Once payment is received, we begin production. Cancellations after payment will result in a 50% refund of the amount paid.
