How to Measure & Order (cm)

  1. Use a soft measuring tape and measure around your wrist bone in centimeters.
  2. One charm = 1 cm. To get the finished bracelet length, add 1 charm to your wrist measurement.
    Example: If your wrist is 19 cm, order 20 charms.
  3. Choose a size that allows comfortable movement (adding 1 charm gives a relaxed fit).

Care & Maintenance Guide

Keep your charms beautiful for years to come

Cleaning

  • Wipe charms with a soft microfiber cloth after wear
  • For deeper cleaning, use warm water with mild soap and a soft brush
  • Rinse and dry thoroughly â€” avoid soaking in harsh chemicals
  • Do not use bleach, abrasive cleaners, or polishing compounds

Storage

  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Keep in a soft pouch or airtight container to reduce dust exposure
  • Separate different metals and delicate pieces to prevent scratches
  • Avoid storing in humid areas like bathrooms

Handling

  • Remove your bracelet before swimming in pools or hot tubs
  • Avoid direct contact with perfumes and strong chemicals
  • Take off during sports or heavy manual work to prevent damage
  • Be gentle when sliding charms to rearrange them
